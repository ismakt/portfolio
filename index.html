Revolut - Churn Analysis

View Full PDF
PDF

Final solo evaluated project completed during the Data Analytics Bootcamp at Le Wagon.


This project focused on a Customer Engagement and Churn Analysis for Revolut, aiming to understand user retention in a context of high customer acquisition costs.


The analysis examined churn drivers, engagement patterns, communication channel efficiency, geographic and usage segmentation, including spending behavior and crypto adoption across age groups.


It also assessed user tendencies toward subscription upgrades to higher paid tiers.


Required skills:
SQL, BigQuery, Looker, Churn Analysis, Data Segmentation.

Zus Coffee - Sales Analysis

Open Dashboard
Dashboard

The ZUS Coffee International Sales Analysis project focused on building an automated Excel dashboard consolidating international sales data for a large coffee company operating across multiple countries.


The objective was to centralize and structure dispersed sales information in order to enable clear analysis of performance across different geographic regions and distribution channels.


The dashboard allows identification of top-performing clients by location, analysis of best-selling coffee products, and tracking of sales evolution over time to support strategic decision-making.


This tool provides a continuous monitoring system to follow business performance and improve long-term sales insights at an international scale.


Required skills:
Excel, Data Analysis, Dashboard Design.

UK Road Safety

UK Road Safety Analysis project focused on the exploration of a large database of road incidents across the United Kingdom.


An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to enable dynamic navigation through accident data, with filtering by incident type and road conditions across the entire UK.


The objective was to extract analytical insights, identify key patterns, and support prevention-oriented decision-making through data visualization.


Required skills:
Power BI, SQL.
